Our Events

Independent MTB Cycling Race Mulleriyawa 2022

20th of March 2022

Independent Sports Club MTB Race Mulleriyawa, which was organized for the first time by the Independent sports club, was held on the 20th of March 2022 at Mulleriyawa Chandrika Kumaratunga Rd. The competition was held under the supervision of the Sri Lanka Cycle Federation. The first place in the competition was won by Sadun Chamika of Fun Bee Sports Club, the second place was won by Gihan Pushpakumara of Sri Lanka Port Authority and the third place was won by Dane Nugera of Sri Lanka Air Force.

Independent MTB Cycling Race Mulleriyawa 2023

2nd of April 2023

Independent Sports Club MTB Race Mulleriyawa, which was organized for the second time by the Independent sports club, was held on the 2nd of April 2023 at Mulleriyawa Chandrika Kumaratunga Rd. The competition was held under the supervision of the Sri Lanka Cycle Federation. The first place in the competition was won by Gihan Pushpakumara of Sri Lanka Port Authority, the second place was won by Dane Nugera of Sri Lanka Air Force and the third place was won by Priyantha Wijesingha of DMMC.
